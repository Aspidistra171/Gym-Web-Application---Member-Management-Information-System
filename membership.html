Common Questions

Need To Know

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All monthly plans can be cancelled with 30 days' notice. Annual plans include a 14-day money-back guarantee.

Do you offer student discounts?

Absolutely. Students get 20% off any monthly plan with valid ID. Just show it during registration.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze for up to 3 months per year at no cost. Useful for travel or injury recovery.

Is there a sign-up fee?

No sign-up fees, ever. The price you see is what you pay. Just membership — clean and simple.
